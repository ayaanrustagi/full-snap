Privacy

Full Snap is local-first. This page is the whole story.

Full Snap uses activeTab and scripting to scroll and capture the visible tab, then opens a local editor for annotation.

Capture data is held briefly in chrome.storage.session so the editor tab can stitch and draw. When you download a PNG, the file is written to your machine only.

There are no Full Snap servers. No account. No analytics SDKs in the extension for selling data. We do not upload page contents, screenshots, or annotations to a third party as part of the product.

Restricted Chrome pages (chrome://, Web Store, etc.) cannot be scripted by extensions; Full Snap targets normal http(s) websites.

Your privacy is
non-negotiable.

Full Snap is built to capture pages, not to watch you browse. Read the privacy policy.

Nothing uploads

Capture buffers live in session storage for the editor tab. There is no Full Snap server.

You’re always in control

It only runs when you click Capture. No background screenshots, no auto-sync.

Scoped permissions

Uses activeTab + scripting for the capture flow. Normal http(s) pages only — not chrome:// internals.

Full Snap follows Chrome Web Store developer policies and best practices.
